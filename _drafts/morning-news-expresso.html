Interesting story on Newsforge about the current state of cryptographic hash algorithms.

Crypographic hash functions are sometimes very useful. Even if you don't need all the security they provide for encryption purposes (fingerprints), they are really nice when you just need a hash function in your program (they are just popular hash functions after all). Why writing one from scratch?. Cache subsystems are a good example.
